hi, i'm abby,

i'm a filipino-american creative based in the san francisco bay area. i have a background in music and love connecting with people to find out what they are passionate about.

i was initially drawn to web development by my early days as a tumblr blogger who would "hack the system" to make cosmetic changes to my home page. I am fascinated by coding's complexity and how it combines creativity and logical problem solving. Coding reminds me of music theory, in many ways!

in my free time i enjoy sitting out in the sun with something sweet to drink and a fantasy novel to read.
